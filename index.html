Act It Out

Creating an empowering learning environment for underprivileged children while teaching them to express themselves through theatre and further develop their skills (specifically their English speaking skills).

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a safe and inclusive space where children can learn the skills they need to succeed, both on and off the stage. We believe that theatre is a powerful way for children to develop their English speaking skills, while also learning valuable life skills, like self-discipline, teamwork, and empathy. With experienced instructors and a variety of programs designed for different skill levels, we are dedicated to providing theatre that are accessible to everyone.

What we offer

Our Online Classes are designed to help you learn the basics of drama from the comfort of your home. We offer online Google Meet classes that cover a range of topics from improv to monologues. Our experienced instructors will guide you through each class, ensuring that you get the most out of your time with us.

About Us

For us, it has always been about making a difference. We recognized the value of a quality education from an early age, and were able to see how the performing arts could help bring that message to life for others. By starting Act It Out we have created a platform to share our passion with our community, and to help educate young people about the importance of education and creativity. We provide online workshops that combine the best of both theatre and quality education.
